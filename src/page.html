Frequently Asked Questions


Q. I have an idea but have not implemented it yet, can I still apply?
Yes. It’s recommended that you have a core team assembled already that is able to carry out your idea and clear articulation on your path forward prior to applying. Remember that we are looking to fund the best crypto projects and founders and companies will be evaluated against that bar.

Q. Can I attend the event in a virtual capacity?
If your project is selected to pitch, we will require your attendance in person. The pitch day will be held in New York on 23rd September 2022.

Q. What industry/ sector are the startups from?
Web3 Startups from all sectors and industries are encouraged to join.

Q. Do I have to build on a specific chain to be eligible?
No, we support builders building on all chains, with brownie points for projects with inter-chain operability.

Q. How can I improve my chances of securing funding?
Consistent weekly updates on your buidl.so startup profile goes a long way in showing a founder's perseverance. Proof of progress is at the center of our evaluation process.

Q. Will I receive any travel sponsorship?
Unfortunately not. Founders who are selected will have to make travel arrangements on their own.
